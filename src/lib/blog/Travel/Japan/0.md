# How to get Travel Wallet Card in GS25

A step-by-step guide for travelers.

Planning a trip to Japan? Here's how to get a convenient travel wallet card at GS25 convenience stores.

## What You Need

- Passport
- Cash (KRW)
- About 5 minutes

## Steps

1. Find a GS25 with the travel card service
2. Tell the staff "Travel Wallet Card please"
3. Fill out the simple form
4. Load money onto the card
5. Done!

## Benefits

- No bank account needed
- Can be used in Japan
- Better exchange rates than cash
- Convenient and safe

This saved me so much hassle on my last trip.
